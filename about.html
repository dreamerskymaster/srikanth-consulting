The practitioner

Two decades inside the hiring machine.

Srikanth has led HR operations, talent acquisition, performance management, employee engagement, learning and development, compensation and benefits, compliance, and HR digitalisation across leading manufacturing and engineering organisations. He has aligned human-capital strategy with business goals, driven organisational effectiveness, and built high-performance workplace cultures.

A strong advocate of digital transformation, he has led implementations of HRIS, SuccessFactors, and HCM platforms, improving process automation, efficiency, and the everyday employee experience. Across his career he has delivered measurable impact through workforce optimisation, policy innovation, and employee wellbeing.

Beyond corporate leadership, he is deeply committed to mentoring professionals, promoting mindful leadership, and building positive workplace ecosystems, the conviction that now drives his campus-to-corporate work.
