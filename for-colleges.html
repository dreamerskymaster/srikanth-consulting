The placement officer's reality

The technical box is ticked. The rest decides the offer.

Recruiters rarely reject your students on aptitude. They reject on the interview, hesitant communication, weak group discussions, visible nerves, and a gap in professional maturity. That is coachable, but only through practice, and only if someone in the room has actually sat on the other side of the hiring table.

  • Students who freeze in interviews despite strong marks
  • Group discussions that go quiet or turn chaotic
  • Placement statistics that plateau year after year
  • Accreditation narratives that need evidence, not adjectives
